Ducati Scrambler 800

Ducati Scrambler 800 is a 803cc cafe racer bike in a price range of Rs. 10.39 Lakh to 12.00 Lakh in India. Scrambler 800 gives a mileage of 19 kmpl. It gets disc brakes in the front and rear, apart from this it weighs 191 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13.5 liters. The bike has a six speed gearbox. It is available in 3 variants and 9 beautiful colours.
